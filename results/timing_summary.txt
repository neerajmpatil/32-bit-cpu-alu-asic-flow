Post-Place-and-Route Frequency Summary
======================================

This summary compares the maximum post-place-and-route frequencies
reported for the 32-bit CPU/ALU design using different adder architectures.

Adder Architecture       Maximum Frequency
------------------       -----------------
Carry Ripple Adder       160.000 MHz
Carry Lookahead Adder    157.870 MHz
Carry Skip Adder         163.180 MHz
Carry Select Adder       160.102 MHz

Observation:
The Carry Skip Adder achieved the highest reported maximum frequency
among the evaluated architectures. The results show that adder choice
affects timing performance after physical implementation, even when the
higher-level CPU/ALU datapath remains consistent.
